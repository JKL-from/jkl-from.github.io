About Me

Professor Ji Xiaoqiang earned his Ph.D. from Columbia University in the United States. He currently serves as an Assistant Professor and Graduate Supervisor in the School of Science and Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, Deputy Director of the Guangdong Provincial Engineering Technology Research Center for Embodied Intelligent Robotics, Committee Member of the Intelligent Internet of Things Special Committee of the Chinese Society of Simulation Technology, and Chief Scientist of the ASEAN-China Artificial Intelligence Laboratory.
